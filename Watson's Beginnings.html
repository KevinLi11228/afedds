Watson's Beginnings

Chris Watson was born in a ship in Valparaiso, Chile, given birth by his Irish Mother and his German-Chilean father. When he was 2, he migrated with his family to New Zealand. So he grew up in New Zealand, and attempted school in Oamaru.


When he was 10, he left school and worked as a railway constructor and a printing assistant, more further, he became an apprenticed compositor for the North Otago Times


When he was 19, he migrated to Sydney, and that's where he will be involved in politics, and find his way to success.
